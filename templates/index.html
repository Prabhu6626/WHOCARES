How to Make Organic Fertilizers and Pesticides

Organic Fertilizers

1. Compost

Materials Needed: Kitchen scraps, yard waste.

Steps: 1. Collect materials and layer them in a compost bin. 2. Turn the pile every few weeks. 3. Wait 2-6 months until it turns into dark compost.

2. Banana Peel Fertilizer

Materials Needed: Banana peels, water.

Steps: 1. Chop banana peels and soak in water for 24-48 hours. 2. Use the nutrient-rich water for watering plants.

3. Eggshell Fertilizer

Materials Needed: Eggshells.

Steps: 1. Rinse, dry, and crush eggshells into a powder. 2. Sprinkle around plants for calcium enrichment.

Organic Pesticides

1. Neem Oil Spray

Materials Needed: Neem oil, water, liquid soap.

Steps: 1. Mix neem oil, soap, and water. 2. Spray on plants every 7-14 days.

2. Garlic Spray

Materials Needed: Garlic, water, liquid soap.

Steps: 1. Chop garlic and infuse in water overnight. 2. Strain, mix with soap, and spray on plants.

3. Soap and Water Solution

Materials Needed: Liquid soap, water.

Steps: 1. Mix soap with water. 2. Spray directly on pests.
